    About

    This shoe is a therapeutic shoe meant for induviduals with constant leg pain that limits their mobility and exercise. The massage feature of this shoe helps users rest their feet when they have pain, with the help of 3 powerful vibrating motors, while the diagnose feature detects pain, using a special algorithm that involvs the X, Y, and Z, as well as pessure sensors and an accelerometer/gyroscope module. Finally, the heat feature uses a 5 x 10 heatpad. Users can control voltage coming to this pad and adjust the heatpad to be on or off.
